Use this tool to compare the body and viewport sizes of 1x weapon sight attachments. Reference screenshots and the backdrops are taken in the Test Server for Operation Grim Sky.

NATO vs. Russian sights

The NATO sights can be equipped on most guns, while Russian operators (i.e. Kapkan, Tachanka, Glaz, Fuze, and Finka) feature a unique set of Russian sights.
